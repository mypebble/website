Keeping track of every penny

Schools, academies and local authorities manage their voluntary income more effectively and efficiently with Fund Manager. It’s not just for schools – charities, churches and clubs also enjoy the benefits.

Fund Manager can accurately record, report and bank cash, cheques and online payments for voluntary income like trips, uniform, dinner money and club fees. Now cloud hosted, it gives you the flexibility to securely access your accounts at any internet-enabled computer.

We pride ourselves on being there for our customers when it matters most. As a result, we offer unlimited telephone and remote access support as part of your license.

Record Transactions

Fund Manager allows you to record receipts and payments accurately and responsibly with the option of printable receipts.

  • Record monies against individual names for items like dinner money, trips and entry fees.
  • Give yourself the ability to enter bulk payments.
  • Keep an audit trail for all payments and cancellations, allowing you to relax in the knowledge you are performing your role responsibly.

Create Reports

From simple reports that provide your balance at a glance to in-depth reports for audit, Fund Manager gives you total flexibility.

  • Customise the exact report you want to see with easy access to all transactions within your accounts.
  • Find discrepancies like double entries by searching for transactions by date, type and value.
  • Track the outstanding balance of items that are paid for in instalments.
  • Create a report of transactions for an individual if requested.
  • Close down your financial year with all the detailed reports you need by using our incredibly simple period end process.

Register & Dinner Money

Ideal for keeping an eye on unpaid dinner money and entry fees, our innovative Fund Manager Register App allows organisations to track items taken in real time.

  • You can use the Register App to instantly record receipt of a multitude of fixed price items – just create an item and unit cost.
  • Using a handheld tablet, tick to confirm an individual has taken a paid (or free) item and their account is instantly deducted your customisable fee.
  • Manage a balance, giving you the option to inform parents or individuals when they are low or in arrears.

Banking & Reconciliation

Fund Manager gives you the ability to record all of your banking processes with a few easy to use screens.

  • Simply record paying in slips by selecting cash and cheques that have been taken to the bank.
  • Reconciliation is as easy as ticking off entries against your paper statement through to an end balance.
  • Take a look at reconciliations from previous months and print them at any time.
  • Each process is followed by an automatically generated report for you to print and add to your records.

Online Payments

In partnership with various providers, our online payments module enables easy integration, allowing you to take online payments for all of your items.

  • Create items available for payment in a number of simple steps; choose a description, value and deadline. You could even allow individuals to make a contribution at an amount they are comfortable with.
  • Payments are fast, secure and the whole process significantly reduces the administration time required by minimising the number of cash and cheque payments that need to be manually handled.
  • Import your transactions in seconds so they are added to your reports and banking.
  • Keep everything together in one solution.

Training & Support

We’ll give you the training and support you need.

  • As a new customer you receive online training to help you get started. We make sure you’ll make the most of the system to meet your needs.
  • On-site support can be arranged for those wanting a detailed hands-on session in the work place, ideal for individuals and groups of trainees.
  • Each customer has the continued support of Pebble’s experienced technical support team, available by telephone or email – whatever best suits your way of working.
  • You can also grant us access to your system for remote support; we’ll resolve any problem you have while you wait.
