Welcome

We create simple and intuitive software to help schools and academies, local authorities and caterers, manage their voluntary income more effectively and efficiently.

With Fund Manager, you can accurately record, report and bank cash, cheques and online payments for voluntary income like trips, uniform, dinner money and club fees. Now cloud based, Fund Manager also gives you the flexibility to securely access their accounts at any internet-enabled computer.

We pride ourselves in being there for our customers when it matters most. As a result, we offer unlimited telephone and remote access support as part of your license.

Recort Transactions

Fund Manager allows you to record receipts and payments accurately and responsibly with the option of printable receipts.

  • Record monies against individual names for items like dinner money, trips and entry fees.
  • Give yourself the ability to enter bulk payments, streamlining the process of inputting batches of identical cheques for example.
  • Keep an audit trail for all payments and cancellations, allowing you to relax in the knowledge you are performing your role responsibly.

Create Reports

From simple reports that provide your balance at a glance to in-depth reports for audit, Fund Manager gives you total flexibility.

  • Customise the exact report you want to see with easy access to all transactions within your accounts.
  • Find discrepancies like double entries by searching for transactions by date, type and value.
  • Track the outstanding balance of items that are paid for in instalments.
  • Create a report of transactions for an individual if requested.
  • Close down your financial year with all the detailed reports you need by using our incredibly simple period end process.

Banking & Reconciliation

Fund Manager gives you the ability to record all of your banking processes with a few easy to use screens.

  • Simply record paying in slips by selecting cash and cheques that have been taken to the bank.
  • Reconciliation is as easy as ticking off entries against your paper statement through to an end balance.
  • Take a look at reconciliations from previous months and print them at any time.

Online Payments

In partnership with various providers, our online payments module enables easy integration, allowing you to take online payments for all of your items.

  • Create items available for payment in a number of simple steps; choose a description, value and deadline. You could even allow individuals to make a contribution at an amount they are comfortable with.
  • Payments are fast, secure and the whole process significantly reduces the administration time required by minimising the number of cash and cheque payments that need to be manually handled.
  • Import your transactions in seconds so they are added to your reports and banking.
  • Keep everything together in one solution.
