Pebble Supports Make a Wish Foundation in 2013

We’re delighted to announce our chosen charity for 2013 is the Make a Wish Foundation. We’ll be fundraising by sharing the physical challenge of a triathlon – with some of us swimming, others cycling, and the rest of us running, to clock up the miles and the sponsorship. We’ll also be holding a charity music event in the summer with local bands – including our very own Dan Wakenshaw – right here in Newcastle-upon-Tyne. More details to follow!

2012 Miles for Charity

We challenged ourselves to cycle 2012 miles to raise £2,012 for ChildLine over the summer. We had a fantastic time on our bike ride to Tynemouth on the 10th June, cycling from Pebble HQ in Ouseburn, Newcastle, along the cycle route to the coast.

Thank you to everyone who sponsored us and supported this worthwhile cause.
