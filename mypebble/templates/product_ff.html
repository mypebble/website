Grants for UK schools made easy

We’re passionate about fundraising and believe in helping schools achieve their funding potential. We want to share our knowledge and experience with you to identify funding opportunities and write winning applications to make a real difference to your school community.

Alongside working with primary, secondary and special schools, we also support academies, local authorities and not-for-profit organisations raise additional funds for educational projects and activities.

Find Grants

School Fund Finder is your comprehensive guide to grants, awards, competitions, in-kind support and free equipment available to UK schools.

  • Updated every week, we give you access to grants from UK government, the European Union, lottery funding, and grant-making trusts and foundations.
  • Hundreds of grants are available for arts & heritage, community engagement, environment, health & sport, ICT, professional development, school partnerships & international links, school buildings & grounds, school trips and special needs education.

Save Time

To save you spending hours on sporadic searching using generic search engines, we do all the work for you to provide you with a time efficient service tailored to your specific needs.

  • We are constantly researching new grants, adding them to the website as soon as they become available.
  • Using the grant search, you can define your school type and location, meaning you will only find opportunities you are eligible for.
  • Refine your search by project type or curriculum subject to find grants for everything you need; from ICT equipment to solar panels and library books to outdoor play.

Develop Skills

We’ll help you develop the practical skills you need for a bright funding future, whether you are new to fundraising or a seasoned fundraiser looking for support.

  • Use our documents section to take you step by step through every stage of the application process to help you write a successful grant application.
  • Download and print templates to help you plan your budget, develop an effective project plan and create a timetable of activities to achieve your fundraising goals.
  • Read our fundraising factsheets to give you a comprehensive guide to school fundraising; from holding fundraising events to using online fundraising tools.

Get Advice

We’ll give you the support you need – from finding funding to writing a winning application. Just think of us as an additional member of your fundraising team.

  • Talk to us or send an email; our experienced fundraisers are here to help you.
  • Receive personalised feedback on any application you make; just to make sure that it’s the very best it can be.
  • Join us for an online fundraising tutorial; we’ll show you how to get the most out of your School Fund Finder experience.

P.S. Our support is unlimited and we don’t take an additional fee from any grants you are awarded.

Keep Informed

Make sure you don’t miss out on a funding opportunity ever again!

  • Customise your email alerts and we’ll notify you when new grants are added to the website that match your funding objectives.
  • Receive our monthly newsletters packed full of funding news and advice, new grants and deadline reminders.
  • Leave a message and share ideas with fellow School Fund Finders on our Fundraising Wall.

Training Days

Ask us about our fundraising workshops and training days; we’ll come to you to share our knowledge and develop your practical skills to maximise your funding success.

  • Led by our team of experienced fundraisers, we’ll give you the knowledge and skills you need to become confident and successful fundraisers.
  • We offer a tailor-made programme to meet the needs of your individual school or cluster; from an introduction to fundraising to developing your bid writing skills.
  • We also work with local authorities and national associations, delivering keynote speeches and fundraising workshops at conferences.
